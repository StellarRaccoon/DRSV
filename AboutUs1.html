About Us

Disaster Ready Chippewa Valley, Inc., is a tax exempt, non-profit organization formed exclusively for charitable and educational purposes as defined in Section 501(c)3 of the Internal Revenue Code. Disaster Ready Chippewa Valley, Inc., was incorporated in the State of Wisconsin in April 2011.

  1. Goal 1: To sustain a strong, effective organization to guide, coordinate, and implement project activities.
  2. Goal 2: To increase private-sector awareness of disaster threats, provide businesses with the tools to mitigate their risks, and advocate for an efficient system of distributing goods, services, and other resources to the community and businesses in times of a disaster.
  3. Goal 3: Strengthen private-sector involvement and communication with government emergency planning and response entities.
Motto: If Chippewa Valley businesses are prepared for a disaster, then our communities are better prepared.
