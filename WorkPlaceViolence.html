Workplace Violence Seminars
DRCV, in conjunction with the City of Eau Claire Police Department, OSHA, and Eau Claire County Emergency Management, have hosted two workplace violence seminars in May 2013 and November 2015. A third event in partnership with Eau Claire Policy Dept., CTVC, and OSHA was held in May 2018. All events were very well attended, demonstrating strong, ongoing interest in this topic.
